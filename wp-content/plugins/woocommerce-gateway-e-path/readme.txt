== Description ==

Integrate your WooCommerce store with the e-Path payment gateway.

This WooCommerce extension is available for purchase on [woocommerce.com](https://woocommerce.com/products/e-path-gateway/).

The [e-Path](https://e-path.com.au/) service allows you to collect your customer's credit card details and then manually process payments offline.

No SSL certificate is required because the credit card handling is all performed on the e-Path website.
